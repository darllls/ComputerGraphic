Dive into Fractals, Colors and Geometry

explore colorful transformations and dynamic shapes

This application is a versatile tool that seamlessly combines mathematical intricacy with artistic creativity. Its primary functions encompass the generation of captivating fractal images, including the visually striking "Universal Closed Line of Koch" fractal. Users have the freedom to explore various facets of these fractals, adjusting parameters such as the base figure (triangle, square) and the ratio that defines the division of the sides of this figure into segments (resulting in a midsegment equilateral triangle without a base). Users can generate images for different numbers of iterations, providing endless artistic possibilities.

In addition to fractal art, the app also features essential color models, namely CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Key/Black) and HSV (Hue, Saturation, Value). This allows artists and designers to exercise precise control over colors, facilitating the creation of vibrant and harmonious color schemes. For instance, users can effortlessly fine-tune the saturation of the purple color and its complementary color, resulting in dynamic and visually appealing color variations

Furthermore, the app offers a unique animation function. Users can input the coordinates of the base of an isosceles triangle and its height, allowing for the creation of a dynamic animation along the trajectory defined by the equation Ax + By + C = 0, where the coefficients A, B, and C are user-defined. This adds an element of motion and transformation to geometric elements within fractal art, making it a truly immersive and dynamic experience.
